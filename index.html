Yoga for Climbers

This class is perfect for anyone who wants to work on flexibility and improve their overall performance. The areas that we mainly focus on are hips, hamstrings, chest and shoulders to help you with wide bridging, high feet, balance, powerful heel hooks, open and stable shoulders for gastons. Suitable for beginners, and not just for climbers, however, some experience in fitness is beneficial, expect to feel worked out.

DariYoga

Philosophy

I believe that yoga is not to be a luxurious hobby available only to the lucky few but open to all - that is why I offer classes just for £6. You are invited to drop in whenever you can - there’s no hidden tricks/time limits to use your passes either, just show up! The classes are mixed ability meaning we take each pose from it’s basic form to the advanced variations for those who are up for it. I believe in hands-on assists when relevant, but it’s okay if you are not keen on those - I will ask before class. Namaste x

DariYoga

About me

My name is Daria. I am from Moscow, and I moved to Harrogate in 2014. My yoga journey began in 2007 at the Indian Embassy in Moscow with Dr. Suresh Babu and carried on at various places but most importantly - at Prana Yoga school where it had gone up to a new level of dedication and depth. When I moved to Yorkshire I had already decided to get my Yoga Teacher Training done and was looking for an opportunity and a perfect course to do it. I got my qualification in May 2017 in France, taught by RYS Quaternity Yoga. My two other huge passions are books and climbing. You can read more about me here.
